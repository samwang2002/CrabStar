Crab Star is a chess engine written by Sam Wang and Justin Peng. It uses a combination of a neural network and hardcoded factors (e.g. material score) to evaluate positions. Its strength in blitz chess is around 2300 elo.

The neural network was first trained over a dataset of 500,000 positions along with their Stockfish evaluations, and then improved by playing against itself over 30,000 times.
